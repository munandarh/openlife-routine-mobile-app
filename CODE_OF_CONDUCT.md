# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Give technical feedback without hostility.
- Keep discussion focused on the product and code.
- Assume good intent, verify with evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Repeated bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove abusive contributions or discussions.
